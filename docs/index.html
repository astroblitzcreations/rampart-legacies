About the Game

Classic fortress combat, rebuilt for a new generation

Rampart Legacies combines strategic wall construction, cannon placement, territorial pressure, and castle survival into a fast, high-stakes battle loop. Inspired by classic fortress warfare, it is being rebuilt with modern online infrastructure, stronger visual identity, and room to grow into a full competitive multiplayer title.
